ArtWest

Logo design, website design, print materials

I had the opportunity to help brand a new non-profit, ArtWest.

Client RFP: Clean, modern design which accurately reflected the contemporary artwork created in western Africa.

Scope of work: Logo design, website design, and print materials. Testing and CSS modifications after development. Visit the completed website at myartwest.com

Logo exploration

Set of logo exploration submitted for internal team review. 10 final logos from the team were sent to client for approval.

Final logo design

Refined client's logo choice and color exploration. Client requested a largely neutral, but not grayscale, color palette. Created vertical, horizontal, tagline, and reverse logo types.

First round web design

Client requested simple, clean website design that used minimal photography as a majority of the work was not to be photographed. I used colorblocking and typography to create interest

Second round mockups

High-fidelity mockups. Included more white space this time, subtly incorporated the ArtWest logo into the design of the site, and introduced a cleaner header.

Final homepage and artist page design

Final homepage and artist page design. Please note: I completed full Photoshop files and HTML/CSS styleguide to send to developer, as this website was developed into a Wordpress theme. To see full current site, please visit myartwest.com

Tri-fold sponsorship brochure. To see full current site, please visit myartwest.com
