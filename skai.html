Skai

Mobile app prototype + responsive website

Skai was a full-fledged semester-long project at Minneapolis College. My group and I had the opportunity to draft a RFP as a team for a group in class. After approval of the RFP, the group work ended, and as continued to develop the brand as individuals.

Client RFP: Clean, modern design which accurately reflected the contemporary artwork created in western Africa.

Scope of work: RFP, Research and development, marketing, branding, style guide, brand indentity, logo design, UX/UI, and front-end development. Book your drone flight now and check out the final website!
