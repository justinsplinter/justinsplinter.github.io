Chameleon Consortium

Logo design, website design, posters, social media

The Chameleon Consortium is a private-public group dedicated to the experiential and economic vitality of downtown Minneapolis with the purpose of creating vibrant downtown experiences by transforming empty retail spaces into equity opportunities for minority- and women-owned businesses.

Client: mpls downtown council

Client RFP: Logo design representative of the city of Minneapolis an the diversity of the vendors to be featured in pop-up shops.

Scope of work: Logo design, website design, brand posters, social media imagery.

External links: Visit Chameleon Consortium and Chameleon Shoppes websites.
