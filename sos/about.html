About SOS Theater

Mission Statement

SOS Theater is a new production company dedicated to producing evocative works that present fresh and entertaining perspectives of this ludicrous thing called life.

SOS Theater: Seven goals

one

Goal One

To bring theater that brings "Minnesota Nice" to life.

two

Goal Two

To create opportunities for the huge pool of honestly talented Minnesotan actors.

three

Goal Three

To bring new theater-goers to a theater that tells their stories.

four

Goal Four

To help promote stories of complex and non-stereotypical women.

five

Goal Five

To help promote stories of complex and non-stereotypical men.

six

Goal Six

To help dispel 'pretentious' as a word defining theater.

seven

Goal Seven

To entertain.
