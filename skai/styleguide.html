Style guide 2019

Brand Personality

Brand personality is an expression of who we are. The Skai logo conveys aspirational confidence and simplicity. The emphasis of the crossbar in the letter A, points upwards, not only towards the sky, but towards prosperous possibilities.

Logo

In clockwise order: standard logo, grayscale, sub-branded, and reversed. Our logo is our identity, which represents our brand, and what we stand for. Therefore, never alter the proportion, the colors, or the spacing of the logo. The Skai logo always requires a minimum of 25 pixels of margin around all sides.

Color Palette

These 6 colors are the only colors to be used in any Skai print or web application. The first color block represents 100% intensity, and then 60% and 20% opacity. 20% blocks are to be used for background color blocks, and 60% intensity can be used for buttons, color bars, or highlighted text.
